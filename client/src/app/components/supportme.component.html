Introducing Veracity

Your handy news analysis companion

Disgruntled by the proliferation of biased news from untrustworthy sources, Veracity was built to give comsumers a handy tool to perform a quick analysis on new articles before hitting the send button which sends the article along with the analysis.

The algorithm categorizes news content into 2 labels; real and notreal. Notreal would include satire, opinion pieces, fake news, and everything else that wasnâ€™t written in a purely factual way that also adhered to the AP standards.

Future updates will allow users to make their own classifications like how one classifies spam mails today. Learn more about AP values and princples here.
